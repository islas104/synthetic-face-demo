# Responsible Use Policy

This project exists to **educate**, not to enable harm.

## Permitted uses

- Academic research into synthetic media
- Security and media literacy demonstrations
- Building deepfake detection tools
- Journalism and awareness campaigns (with disclosure)

## Prohibited uses

- Creating synthetic media of real people without their written consent
- Impersonating individuals to deceive others
- Non-consensual intimate imagery (NCII) — this is illegal in most jurisdictions
- Political disinformation or election interference
- Fraud, extortion, or harassment

## Your responsibilities

By running this software you agree that:

1. You have obtained consent from any person whose likeness you use.
2. You will clearly label all outputs as synthetic/AI-generated.
3. You will not attempt to remove or bypass the embedded watermark.
4. You will comply with all applicable laws in your jurisdiction.

## Reporting misuse

If you see this tool being used to harm someone, report it to:
- The platform hosting the content
- Your local law enforcement
- The Internet Watch Foundation (IWF): https://www.iwf.org.uk

## Legal notice

The authors disclaim all liability for misuse. Generating synthetic media of a person without consent may violate GDPR, CCPA, the UK Online Safety Act, and various state/national laws.
